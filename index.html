Newborn Tracker

The purpose of the web application is to provide a tracking tool that
mothers can use to keep track of important factors such as feedings and times, bowel movements, and wet diapers.

In order for this application to work properly, it will need access to your local storage
in order to save all of the data you record within. It is important to know that this data remains
with you on your computer and is not used by or accessed by any other. If you clear your local storage
in your browser you will loose all of your data. In addition, you should always use the same browser as
you data will only be saved in the browser you are currently using.

I hope this web application proves useful and congratulations on your new addition!
